Frequently Asked Questions

Each standard driving lesson for Codes 08 and 10 is 60 minutes (1 hour) long. For Code 14, lessons are typically longer to accommodate the complexity of the vehicle and maneuvers. We structure our lessons to maximize practical learning time.

The R1000 Learner's Class covers comprehensive theory training on road rules, signs, and vehicle controls for all licence codes (08, 10, and 14). It includes study materials and preparation to ensure you pass the written K53 test on your first try.

The R300 is a once-off charge for our transport service, available to students in Lenasia and surrounding areas. This fee covers picking you up and dropping you off for your lessons, making your training process hassle-free and convenient.

The R2,500.00 is an initial deposit required to secure your full package slot and lessons. This amount is subtracted from the final package price. It is non-refundable but ensures your place in our training schedule.
